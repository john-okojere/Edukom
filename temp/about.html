ABOUT EDUKOM

Edukom is an online tutoring platform offering personalized and flexible education for students aged 4 to 18. Led by Ope Komolafe, the platform covers a range of subjects following different curricula.

Edukom prepares students for exams, addressing logistical and security concerns through online classes with a team of experienced tutors fully dedicated to empowering students for global and execllence and a lasting legacy.

We offer tutoring services to learners from Reception class to A Level

Classes are predominantly online or virtual, however, exceptions can be made based on extenuating circumstances which necessitate a physical class.

Our online classes are created to provide solutions to the following:

  • Logistic problems of getting a tutor to the class location irrespective of the distance.
  • Limitation of qualified, experienced, and personable Tutors in your city.
  • Security concerns of having strangers in your home with access to your children.
  • Restrictions on scheduling classes when you are out of the country on holiday or visiting family across the country.

Our Method of Instruction

At Educom we teach classes online using virtual platforms to ensure limitless teaching can take place. We use technological tools to share videos for learning support and make use of white boards for subjects/topics requiring calculations or annotations.
